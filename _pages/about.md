About
======
Hello! My name is Tran, and I am a third-year PhD student in the [Department of Linguistics at Cornell University](https://linguistics.cornell.edu/). My research interests include phonetics and phonology, particularly in second language acquisition. Previously, I have investigated English-to-Vietnamese loanword phonology, specifically the process by which speakers adapt lexical stress into tone. My recent work investigates phonetic convergence in L2 learners to better understand the mechanisms underlying L2 speech production and phonetic adaptation over time. I am a member of the [Cornell Phonetics Lab (PLab)](https://conf.ling.cornell.edu/).
